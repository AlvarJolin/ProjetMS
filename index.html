Our team of 8 dedicated members strives to facilitate communication around the world. Thanks to SomaSlate, connections will be easier than ever with people who are deaf and dumb. Our goal is to make conversation simple and effective, bypassing natural barriers that have never been crossed before.

SomaSlate is a live translator for turning sign language into written language. Using python algorithms and video recognition modules, SomaSlate detects the position of the fingers, arms and chest as well as the facial expression of your interlocutor and translates their movements into their equivalent in French.
